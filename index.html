About

I'm a game developer, sonic artist, and folk musicican based in Wellington, New Zealand.

I love creating audio-visual soundscapes, interactive media, and innovating on Celtic music traditions. I enjoy collaborating with other artists and designers to create fun, new games, and unique experiences.
